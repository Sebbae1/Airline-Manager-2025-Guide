About Airline Manager 2025

Airline Manager 2025 is a free-to-play, online, and mobile-based airline simulation game where players build and manage their own airline empire. Players can buy realistic planes, create routes connecting 4000 real airports, and optimize for cargo and passenger transport.
The game features both easy and realistic game modes, with the latter requiring consideration of fackers like runway lengths. Players can also manage staff, participate in marketing campaigns, and compete on global leaderboards.

Aspects

Realisim

The game incorporates realistic elements such as fuel consumption, carbon credits, and airport operations.

In-Depth Management

Players can customize aircraft seating configurations, manage staff, and engage in marketing campaings.

Dynamic Gameplay

The game features real-time economic markets and allows for city and airport expansion.

Multiplayer

Players can compete with friends and other real-life managers on global leaderboards.

Variety

Players can choose from over 400 real airplane models and connect to over 4000 real airports.

Accessibility

The game can be played on smartphones, tablets, and web browsers.
