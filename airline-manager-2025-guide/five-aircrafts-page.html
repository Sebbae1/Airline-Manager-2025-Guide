5. British Aerospace BAE 146-400



Technical Specifications:

  • Cost: $527,912
  • Capacity: 112 pax
  • Runway Requirement: 3,921 feet
  • Fuel Consumption: 17.67 lbs/km
Notes: The British Aerospace Bae 146-300 consumes more fuel than the McDonnell Douglas DC-9-10 at 13.44 lbs/km. The aircraft excels in realism because it is scalable and has low A-Check costs. This plane is ideal for generating steady profit on regional routes.


4. McDonnell Douglas DC-9-10



Technical Specifications:

  • Cost: $659,657
  • Capacity: 90 pax
  • Runway Requirement: 2,000 feet
  • Fuel Consumption: 13.44 lbs/km
  • Range: 2,036 km
Notes: The McDonnell Douglas DC-9-10 is a fan favorite in Airline Manager 2025. The aircraft is an ideal choice for new players looking for a well-rounded and efficient plane. The McDonnell Douglas DC-9-10 is the perfect balance for cost-effectivess and performance in the early stages of your airline.


3. McDonnell Douglas DC-10-10



Technical Specifications:

  • Cost: $6,806,411
  • Capacity: 250 pax
  • Runway Requirement: 2,000 feet
  • Fuel Consumption: 20.33 lbs/km
  • Range: 6,436 km
Notes: The McDonnell Douglas DC-10-10 is a top choice for players in realism mode. This plane is built for profitable long flights. However, its fuel consumption is really high and its speed is not fast.


2. Irkut MC-21-400



Technical Specifications:

  • Cost: $5,204,503
  • Capacity: 230 pax
  • Runway Requirement: 6,800 feet
  • Fuel Consumption: 19.57 lbs/km
  • Range: 5,500 km
  • Cruise Speed: 1,069 kph
Notes: The Irkut MC-21-400 is a fantastic choice for medium-haul routes. The Irkut MC-21-400's fuel consumption is high but its performance and its capacity offsets its fuel consumption, which is great for faster flights and more overall profits.


1. Airbus A380-800



Technical Specifications:

  • Cost: $215,629,503
  • Capacity: 600 pax
  • Runway Requirement: 9,680 feet
  • Fuel Consumption: 22.89 lbs/km
  • Range: 14,500 km
Notes: The Airbus A380-800 is the largest and most powerful aircraft in Airline Manager 2025. The Airbus A380-800 is a titan for long-haul flights and designed for highly-profitable routes. Even though the aircraft has a high fuel consumption, it has outstanding fuel efficiency considering its sheer size.
